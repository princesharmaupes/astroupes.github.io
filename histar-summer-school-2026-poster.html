HiSTAR

Himalayan School for Training in Astronomy Research

A five-day astronomy training school featuring lectures, software labs, group projects, and observational exposure for students and early-career researchers.

Event Dates and Venue

8 to 12 June 2026

UPES, Dehradun and Dolphin (PG) Institute of Biomedical and Natural Sciences (DIBNS)

Priority will be given to students from the Himalayan region, including Uttarakhand, J&K, Himachal, and Ladakh, along with neighboring northern states.

Participation

  • Student participants: 45
  • Faculty participants: 5
  • Speakers and organizers: 10
  • Total expected: 60
Format5-day lectures, labs and projects
FocusObservational astronomy and data analysis
PriorityHimalayan and neighboring northern states
CertificationFull attendance required

Eligibility

  • Final year BSc students in Physics or allied fields
  • MSc students in Physics, Mathematics, Electronics, Astronomy
  • Final year BE/BTech and ME/MTech (Physics or Computer Science)
  • First-year university PhD students

Program Snapshot

  • Morning: Fundamental astronomy, stellar evolution, compact objects, cosmology, instrumentation
  • Afternoon: Python basics, data mining, CMDs, light curves, galaxy morphology
  • Evening: Telescope handling and observational sessions

Logistics

  • Accommodation for resource persons at UPES
  • Subsidized hostel stay for outstation students at DIBNS
  • Daily bus transport between DIBNS and UPES

Travel Support

  • Regional outstation support as per IUCAA norms
  • Local transport for invited speakers and faculty

  • May 15, 2026: Participant selection and confirmation
  • June 8 to 12, 2026: Summer School
