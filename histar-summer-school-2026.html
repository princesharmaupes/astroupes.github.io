Event Overview

HiSTAR Summer School 2026 is designed to strengthen astronomy education and research capacity in the Himalayan and adjoining regions. The proposal describes a structured five-day format with science talks, hands-on projects, software sessions, and group activities.

  • Title: Himalayan School for Training in Astronomy Research (HiSTAR)
  • Dates: June 8-12, 2026
  • Nature: Regional meeting/training school in astronomy
  • Venues: Applied Science Cluster, School of Engineering, UPES (Bidholi, Dehradun) and Dolphin PG Institute, Dehradun
  • Focus: observational, computational, and project-based astronomy training
Science Talks Hands-on Software Group Projects Telescope Session Mentor Interaction
Expected participation breakdown
  • Speakers and organizers: 10 (4 local + 6 outstation)
  • Student participants: 45 (20 local + 25 outstation)
  • Faculty participants: 5 local
