About the Workshop

The Workshop on Data Science in Astronomy aims to introduce students to modern data-driven techniques in astronomical research. The workshop will include invited lectures, tutorials, and hands-on sessions on applications of machine learning, time-series analysis, and large-scale survey data handling.

This workshop is specially designed for Undergraduate (2nd & 3rd year) and Postgraduate students interested in astronomy and data science.

Eligibility

Participants should be:

  • UG (2nd or 3rd year) students in Physics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or related fields.
  • PG (M.Sc./M.Tech) students in Physics, Astronomy, or Data Science.
  • Strong interest in astronomy and basic programming knowledge is desirable.
